Why Bynns

Good food, fair prices, friendly faces

We keep it simple: fresh ingredients, generous fillings, and coffee that won't cost you a fortune. It's the corner spot Dunbar actually needed.

Made to order

Nothing sits under a heat lamp. Your sub, panini or baguette is built fresh when you order it — pile on the veggies, mix your meats, make it yours.

Honestly priced

Drip coffee is $1.75. A latte is $3. In a city where a coffee can run you six bucks, we think a good cup should be something you can grab every day.

Remake guarantee

We trust our coffee and our sandwiches. If yours isn't right, bring it back and we'll remake it — no fuss. That's the whole promise.
